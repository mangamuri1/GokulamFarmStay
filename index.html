AUTHENTIC TRADITIONAL FARMHOUSE EXPERIENCE

Celebrate Every New Beginning with Divine Traditions at Gokulam Farm Stay in Shamirpet, near Hyderabad

Gokulam Farm Stay is a premium luxury farmhouse and event venue located in Shamirpet, near Hyderabad and Gajwel, Telangana. It is designed for families, corporate retreats, and traditional celebrations like weddings and Gruhapravesham. The property features an authentic Manduva-style house, a pristine swimming pool, and extensive indoor and outdoor amenities to accommodate up to 15 overnight guests.

Begin every special occasion with the sacred blessings of Gomatha in the peaceful surroundings of our farm. Experience the beauty of a traditional Gruhapravesham, witness Gomatha entering the home first, and participate in a sacred Homam to preserve timeless heritage.

Surrounded by nature and filled with spiritual warmth, every celebration becomes a memorable journey of culture, peace, prosperity, and togetherness.
